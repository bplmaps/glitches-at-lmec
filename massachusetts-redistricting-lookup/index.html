Massachusetts Redistricting Lookup

Every ten years, after the decennial census, the states are required to redraw the maps of their electoral districts. The Massachusetts General Court has released their proposed maps for the State House and Senate. This tool allows you to search what district an address will belong to if the maps are approved, using data from the proposed new plan for Massachusetts.
